Playgroup

Looking for a fun and welcoming space to spend some quality time with your child/children? Look no further than our Ministry of Education-endorsed playgroup! Our playgroup is the perfect place for parents and caregivers to connect with one another while their little ones play and explore in a safe and supportive environment.

Whether you're a regular attendee or just dropping in for the first time, you're sure to feel at home in our inviting venue. Our playgroups are designed to be informal and relaxed, with plenty of opportunities for socializing and making new friends.

At our playgroup, we believe that every child deserves a chance to play and learn, and we've designed our program to be both fun and educational. With a focus on interactive play and hands-on learning, our playgroups are the perfect place for children to develop their social skills, creativity, and curiosity.

So why wait? Sign up for our Ministry of Education-endorsed playgroup today and experience the joy of connecting with other parents and caregivers while your child/children have fun and make new friends. And remember, anyone is free to join at any time, so come on down and see what we're all about!
Hosted by Karen Fergusson

Our Values

Located in the heart of Miramar, Wellington, The Family Space is a playcare centre that strives to support and strengthen the families in our community. With its focus on building relationships, The Family Space offers a safe and friendly environment where family life is encouraged and nurtured. Our playgroups are informal, relaxed and a great way to meet other families.

Belonging

Providing a warm, friendly and safe environment where family life is encouraged and nurtured.

Outreach

Aims to provide practical services for both children and adults.
Supports families to do their job well, knowing that healthy families are important for growing healthy children.

Faith

Open invitation for families to be part of a faith-based community at our Gateway Church family next door.
All are warmly welcome.

Community

Helping people engage with the wider community.
Accepting families in whatever shape they may come, believing that families are those people who choose to connect together and call themselves a 'family'.

Support

Looking for ways to support and equip parents and caregivers.
Builds families who are healthy, and resilient physically, emotionally, mentally, relationally and spiritually.
Our Venue

The Family Space is a child and family friendly space that is located in the heart of Miramar at 33 Park Road, Miramar, Wellington .

The Family Space is available for hire by groups and organisations that share in the values and objectives that are consistent with our mission: Supporting and Strengthening Families.

There is a zero smoking and alcohol policy.

Please get in touch to discuss terms and conditions and hireage rates.

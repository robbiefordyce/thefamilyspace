Our Mission

Our Core Values

Located in the heart of Miramar, we provide an informal, relaxed, and safe environment designed to support and strengthen families in our community.

Belonging

Providing a warm, safe environment where family life is nurtured and encouraged.

Outreach

Providing practical services for adults and children, supporting you to grow a healthy, strong family.

Faith

An open invitation to be part of our faith-based community next door at Gateway Church. All are warmly welcome.

Community

Embracing families in every shape and size, helping you connect and engage with the wider community.

Support

Equipping parents and caregivers with the tools to build physically, mentally, and relationally healthy families.

Joy

Fostering a playful, positive, and delightfully fun environment where children and families can laugh, learn, and thrive together.

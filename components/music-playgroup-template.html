Music Playgroup

Looking for a fun and educational activity to do with your little one? Look no further than our Ministry of Education-endorsed music group for preschoolers! Led by a highly trained and experienced music teacher, our group is the perfect place for babies and young children to explore the world of music and make new friends.

Whether you're a regular attendee or just dropping in for a casual session, we always welcome new faces with open arms. And if you're new to our group, we invite you to come along with your child/children for a free trial session, so you can see for yourself just how much fun we have!

At our music group, we believe that early exposure to music is essential for a child's development, and we've designed our program to be both fun and educational. With a focus on interactive play and hands-on learning, our sessions are sure to engage and delight children of all ages.

So why wait? Sign up for our music group today and watch as your child blossoms into a confident and creative young musician! And don't forget, pre-registration is always appreciated, but you're welcome to join us on a casual basis as well.

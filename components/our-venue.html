Our Venue

The Family Space is a child and family friendly space that is located in the heart of Miramar at 33 Park Road, Miramar, Wellington .

The Family Space is available for hire by groups and organisations that share in the values and objectives that are consistent with our mission: Supporting and Strengthening Families.

There is a zero smoking and alcohol policy.

Please get in touch to discuss terms and conditions and hireage rates.

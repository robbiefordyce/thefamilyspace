Birthday Parties

The Family Space Center hosts birthday party events for regular attendees (both past and present), providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere for a special day. Our experienced and friendly team is committed to ensuring that every child has a memorable and enjoyable celebration.

Please note that our birthday party events are for children under 5 years old. If you're interested in hosting a party with us, please don't hesitate to get in touch with the Gateway Baptist Church office. We look forward to celebrating with you!
